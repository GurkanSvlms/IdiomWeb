Made for real conversation

Not word-for-word translation.

IdiomSwipe focuses on the phrases that make speech sound natural: everyday idioms, workplace expressions, useful proverbs, and the closest bilingual equivalents when a literal translation is not enough.

Privacy-first by design

Your learning stays on your device.

IdiomSwipe v1 is local-first. There is no account system, tracking, backend, or external API in the app. Learning progress, favorites, review state, and settings are stored locally on device.
