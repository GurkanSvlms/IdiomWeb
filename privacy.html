Privacy Policy

IdiomSwipe is designed as a local-first learning app for English and Turkish expressions. This policy explains what the app stores and what it does not collect.

Information stored on your device

The app may store your learning direction, progress, XP, streaks, favorites, review state, quiz results, daily usage counters, app language preference, and subscription entitlement state locally on your device.

No account, tracking, or backend in v1

IdiomSwipe v1 does not include account registration, a backend service, third-party analytics, advertising tracking, external AI APIs, or advertising identifiers.

Purchases

Subscriptions and purchases are handled by Apple through StoreKit and your Apple ID. IdiomSwipe does not receive or store your payment card details.

Device features

The app may use on-device features such as speech playback, haptics, local notifications if enabled, and local storage for learning progress. These features are used only to provide the app experience.

Deleting data

You can remove local IdiomSwipe data by deleting the app from your device. Some device-level backups or Apple account purchase records may be managed by Apple according to your Apple settings.

Children's privacy

IdiomSwipe does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app is local-first and has no account system in v1, the developer does not operate a user database for the app.

Changes

This policy may be updated when the app adds new features or services. The latest version will be posted on this page.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, use the developer contact method listed on the IdiomSwipe App Store page.
